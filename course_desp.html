The Non-Technical Person's guide to building products & apps

Go from Idea to MVP with no CTO and no coding. Launch your business, app, or product idea cheaply, quickly, and easily.
Instructed by Evan Kimbrell Technology/ Web development

About This Course

Course Description

What are the requirements?

  • Familiarity with basic business and startup concepts is helpful, but not necessary

What am I going to get from this course?

  • Quickly validate their ideas through 5 different techniques
  • Build a landing page that converts through Launchrock
  • Run validation campaigns through Facebook and Google
  • Interpret the results of their validation campaigns and understand basic web analytics
  • Learn to wireframe from scratch with Balsamiq and produce a mockup of YouTube
  • Learn how to determine the best and cheapest way of getting your app designed
  • Learn how to manage design freelancers and to be effective at negotiating a lower price
  • Start a web design contest and receive up to 40 designs free of cost to choose from
  • Make use of free and premium templates to skip the graphic design process entirely
  • Put together a design packet for the design process
  • Find and vet developers from 3 different online sources
  • Test developers with small amounts of investment before committing to them
  • Find the best deals on contractors and save the most money
  • Avoid the common pitfalls of contracting
  • Streamline the communication with their development team
  • Bug test web and mobile applications effectively and professionally
  • Understand the basics of startup traction and how product quality plays into that
  • Leverage your MVP to recruit and attract investment capital
